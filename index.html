Activities

Hackathons

Every year, WCO participates in the Hackathon at RIT judged by representatives from various different companies. Join us there for some fun coding, networking and lots of free food!

Resume Building

Resumes are essential for applying to most jobs, especially in the tech industry. WCO will help you craft the perfect resume to help you land you that internship or job.

Internship Preparation

Internships are a great way to learn and get experience in working in the tech industry. Join WCO to learn about which what internships are available and how to get them!

Coding Exercises

In our increasinly digital world, learning how to code becomes increasingly vital. WCO holds some sessions to get acquainted with a few programming languages such as HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Java, and more.

Collaborations

Computer science is a broad subject which encompasses various different disciplines. WCO collaborates with significant persons and other Oswego clubs to expand your knowledge and interests!

Presentations

WCO holds powerpoint presentations on on how to deal with problems and issues which women have in the tech industry and the work place. Officers also present their topics of interest such as Mobile App building, Natural Language Interface, and more!
